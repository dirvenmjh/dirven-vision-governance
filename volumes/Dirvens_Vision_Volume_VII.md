## The Vision: What Success Looks Like

A governance system that is:

- **Transparent**: Open-source, auditable, verifiable at every level
- **Distributed**: Power diffused across networks, not concentrated in institutions
- **Democratic**: Citizens as participants in real-time, not subjects of quarterly elections
- **Technically Literate**: Designed by engineers and scientists who understand technology's implications
- **Aligned with Markets**: Using prediction markets and incentive structures to reveal truth
- **Protective of Dignity**: Ensuring cognitive integrity and personal autonomy in the age of neural technology
- **Universally Transparent**: If monitoring exists, it exists equally for all—no asymmetry enabling totalitarianism
